Question 9

Farmer Jns has a huge number of cows. He is wondering whether the amount of time a cow spends outside in the sunshine affects how much milk she produces. To test this he makes a controlled experiment where he picks out 20 cows and assigns each a number of hours she should spend outside each day. The experiment runs for a month and Jns records the number of liters of milk each cow produces. Copy-n-paste the following into R and inspect the resulting data frame d.

d <- data.frame(milk = c(685, 691, 476, 1151, 879, 725, 1190, 1107, 809, 539,
                         298, 805, 820, 498, 1026, 1217, 1177, 684, 1061, 834),
                hours = c(3, 7, 6, 10, 6, 5, 10, 11, 9, 3, 6, 6, 3, 5, 8, 11, 
                          12, 9, 5, 5))

Using this data on hours of sunshine and resulting liters of milk Jns wants to know: Does sunshine affect milk production positively or negatively?
